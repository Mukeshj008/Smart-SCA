Understanding Risk Prioritization

Effective Risk combines four factors: (1) CVE Severity from the vulnerability database, (2) Reachability - whether the vulnerable code is actually imported/used in your codebase, (3) Exclusion - whether the dependency is excluded in pom.xml (Maven exclusions), and (4) Exploitability - whether the conditions required to exploit the vulnerability are met. A CRITICAL severity CVE may have LOW effective risk if the dependency is excluded, code is not reachable, or exploit conditions are not met.
